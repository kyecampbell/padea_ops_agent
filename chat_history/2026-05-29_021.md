**Decision 1 (locked): VO model = safety fallback only.**
Selection chain in compose_session_order:
1. Inherent-safe item (item.tags ⊇ student.tags) — unchanged
2. No inherent-safe item → VO fallback: item.tags ⊇ (student.tags − {'vegetarian'}) AND has_vegetarian_option=true → source='dietary_auto_pick', variant='vegetarian_option', safe computed not hardcoded

#### Safety invariant (critical — must not regress)

For VO lines:
- safe=True is computed as: item_tags ⊇ (student_tags − {'vegetarian'})
- NOT hardcoded True. Matches the selection query.
